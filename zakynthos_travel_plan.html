✈ Wednesday - Gentle Arrival & Panoramic Sunset
🌅 Afternoon (After Landing)

Arrive & check into Paradise Beach Hotel

Drive to Town (10 mins) for orientation walk

Late lunch at Base Cafe Bar - waterfront, affordable, great atmosphere

🌅 Evening (6:30pm)

Drive to Bohali Hill (8 mins)

Watch sunset from Bohali Castle Viewpoint with panoramic island views

Dinner at Varkarola - stunning terrace, local wines, moderate prices

🌅 Sunset time: ~7:45pm | Best panoramic viewpoint for first evening orientation
🏖 Thursday - Relaxed Southeast Beach Day (No Long Drives!)
🌅 Morning (9:30am) - RELAXED SOUTHEAST BEACHES

Breakfast at hotel, then drive to Plaka Beach (15 mins)

Plaka Beach - tiny, secluded, crystal clear waters, hardly any tourists

Perfect peaceful morning swim, bring drinks & snacks

☀ Midday - LUNCH OPTIONS

OPTION 1 (Premium): Drive to Nobelos Restaurant (45 mins)

Lunch at Nobelos Seaside Restaurant - premium organic dining, stunning waterfront

Pricing: €50-80/person. Exceptional quality, waterfront tables, worth the splurge!

OPTION 2 (Budget): Stay local at Plaka Beach Taverna (€15-25/person)

🏖️ Afternoon - HIDDEN GEM BEACH

OPTION A: Drive to Dafni Beach (10 mins)

Dafni Beach - wildest beach in Vassilikos, Marine Park protected, crystal clear waters

Why it's perfect: Dirt road access keeps crowds away, rich fish life, hundreds of meters of undisturbed swimming

OPTION B: Hike to Marathias Beach (15 mins)

Marathias Beach - isolated gem, steep hiking path keeps it uncrowded, towering cliffs protection

🌅 Late Afternoon (5:00pm) - STUNNING SUNSET

Drive to Gerakas Beach (8 mins)

Gerakas Beach - pristine sandy beach, crystal clear shallow waters, turtle nesting site

Perfect sunset spot with shallow blue waters, protected environment, much more special than typical beaches

🌊 Ultimate secluded day! Plaka → Dafni/Marathias → Gerakas - from hidden cove to wild protected beach to turtle paradise
🐢 Dafni is Marine Park protected = no boats allowed, hundreds of meters of undisturbed swimming!
🐢 Friday - Boat Cruise & Lighthouse Sunset Dining
🌅 Morning (10:30am)

Leisurely breakfast, then drive to Laganas Marina (20 mins)

Coffee at marina before your 11:50am Boat Cruise (FIXED)

🐢 Your Cruise (11:50am-2:50pm)

Turtle Spotting Cruise - Marathonisi Island, Keri Caves from sea

Swimming stops, turtle watching, boat lunch included

Most relaxing way to see the protected marine area!

🌅 Evening (6:30pm)

Drive to Keri Lighthouse (25 mins)

Sunset dinner at Keri Lighthouse Restaurant - dramatic clifftop dining with sunset views

Pricing: €45-60/person, €35 minimum spend. Book sunset table in advance!

🚤 Perfect cruise timing - your existing booking is perfectly placed
🌅 Keri Lighthouse: €45-60/person with €35 minimum. Spectacular sunset dining - book ahead!
⛵ Saturday - Budget Boat Adventure & Hidden North Coast Beaches
🌅 Morning (9:00am) - BUDGET BOAT RENTAL

Drive to Agios Nikolaos Port (45 mins) - northern boat rental hub

BUDGET OPTIONS:

• Small motor boat - €80-120/half day (4-6 hours, no license needed)

• Shared boat rental - €40-60/person (split costs with others)

• Zodiac/inflatable boat - €60-90/half day

⛵ Morning-Afternoon (10am-4pm) - SECLUDED BEACH EXPLORATION

Korakonisi Beach - stunning arch formation, crystal clear waters, mainly boat accessible

Smugglers Cove (Navagio) - THE famous shipwreck beach, only accessible by boat

Blue Caves - incredible blue water caves, magical swimming spots

Hidden Gems: Explore tiny unnamed coves between cliffs - completely private!

Bring: Snorkeling gear, packed lunch, drinks, sunscreen

🌅 Late Afternoon (4:30pm)

Return boat to Agios Nikolaos Port

Drive to Kampi Village (25 mins)

Kampi Sunset Bar - legendary sunset cocktails after your boat adventure!

Celebrate your amazing day with drinks overlooking the west coast

⛵ BUDGET BOAT DAY: Half-day rental saves money while still accessing the most exclusive beaches!
💰 Save €100+ with budget options: €80-120 total vs €200-300, but same incredible hidden beaches!
🌊 Sunday - South Coast Icons & Farewell
🌅 Morning (9:30am) - HEALING WATERS

Drive to Xigia Beach (40 mins)

Xigia Beach - natural sulfur springs, famous for healing properties

Therapeutic swimming, dramatic white cliffs, unique experience - the water smells but feels amazing!

🌊 Late Morning (11:30am)

Drive to Porto Limnionas (45 mins)

Porto Limnionas - natural swimming pools carved in white limestone

Incredible snorkeling, crystal clear waters, bring water shoes

☀ Midday

Lunch at Porto Limnionas Restaurant - cliff-edge dining, spectacular views

Relaxed afternoon swimming in the natural pools

🌊 Mid-Afternoon (3:30pm) - TINY PARADISE

Drive to Agios Sostis (25 mins)

Agios Sostis Cameo Island - tiny island connected by wooden bridge, crystal clear waters

Perfect for swimming, photos on the famous wooden bridge, very Instagram-worthy!

🌅 Late Afternoon (5:30pm) - MUST SEE FINALE!

Drive to Mizithres Viewpoint (15 mins)

Mizithres Viewpoint - THE ABSOLUTE MUST-SEE! Most iconic viewpoint in all of Greece

Legendary white limestone rocks emerging from turquoise sea - perfect finale sunset

End your trip with the most spectacular view in Zakynthos!

💧 Ultimate finale day: Healing sulfur springs → Natural pools → Tiny paradise island → Most iconic sunset!
⭐ Xigia's sulfur springs are legendary for skin benefits - perfect way to end your beach adventure!

🌺 Paradise Tips for Your Relaxed Adventure

  • All locations chosen for relaxation, beauty, and reasonable prices
  • Maximum 45-minute drives - most are much shorter from your hotel
  • Every day includes at least one spectacular sunset opportunity
  • Restaurant recommendations focus on authentic, good value dining
  • Beach selections offer variety: sandy, rocky, healing, protected
  • Book Kampi sunset bar table in advance for Saturday
  • Bring reef-safe sunscreen for protected turtle areas
  • Pack water shoes for Porto Limnionas rocky areas
  • Download offline maps - some coastal areas have weak signal
  • Ask hotel for wine/picnic recommendations for sunset viewing
  • Keep receipts - many tavernas offer 10% tourist discounts
  • Try local Robola wine - excellent quality, great prices
