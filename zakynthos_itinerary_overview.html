Zakynthos Itinerary Overview

All Locations - 4.5 Days

Wednesday - Gentle Arrival & Panoramic Sunset

  • Paradise Beach Hotel - Your base in Argassi
  • Zakynthos Town - Town center orientation walk
  • Base Cafe Bar - Waterfront lunch restaurant
  • Bohali Castle Viewpoint - Sunset spot with panoramic island views
  • Varkarola Restaurant - Bohali terrace dining

Thursday - Relaxed Southeast Beach Day (No Long Drives!)

  • Plaka Beach - Secluded beach, Vassilikos Peninsula
  • Nobelos Seaside Restaurant - Premium option €50-80/person
  • Plaka Beach Taverna - Budget option €15-25/person
  • Dafni Beach - Wild secluded beach, Marine Park protected
  • Marathias Beach - Alternative isolated cliffside gem
  • Gerakas Beach - Sunset beach, turtle nesting site

Friday - Boat Cruise & Lighthouse Sunset Dining

  • Laganas Marina - Boat cruise departure point
  • Turtle Cruise - 11:50am-2:50pm, Marathonisi Island
  • Keri Caves - Sea caves viewed from boat
  • Keri Lighthouse Restaurant - Sunset dinner €45-60/person

Saturday - Budget Boat Adventure & Hidden North Coast Beaches

  • Agios Nikolaos Port - Northern port, boat rental hub
  • Navagio Beach (Smugglers Cove) - Famous shipwreck beach, boat only access
  • Blue Caves - Magic blue water caves, swimming spots
  • Korakonisi Beach - Stunning arch formation, crystal waters
  • Hidden Coves - Unnamed private beaches between cliffs
  • Kampi Sunset Bar - Legendary sunset cocktails, west coast

Sunday - Ultimate Beach Finale with Healing Waters

  • Xigia Beach - Healing sulfur springs, therapeutic waters
  • Porto Limnionas - Natural swimming pools, white limestone
  • Porto Limnionas Restaurant - Cliff-edge dining, spectacular views
  • Agios Sostis (Cameo Island) - Tiny island, famous wooden bridge
  • Mizithres Viewpoint - MUST SEE, most iconic sunset in Greece

Zakynthos Adventure Summary

A perfect blend of secluded beaches, crystal clear waters, authentic experiences, and iconic must-sees.
